Strategic Briefing: Why Mindset Matters

Moving to proactive solutions can be difficult because the "Standard" path often feels like the responsible choice. However, in a crisis, the benefits of proactivity become clear:

  • Identification Challenge: Standard procedures (manual counts, waiting for ISPs) are familiar, while proactive solutions (predictive analytics, edge failovers) require higher technical readiness.
  • Operational Benefits: Proactive IT moves technology from a reactive "cost center" to a "care enabler." It reduces clinical risk by anticipating problems before they interrupt patient care.
